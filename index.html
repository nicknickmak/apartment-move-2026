Terms Explained

COI (Certificate of Insurance)
A document from your moving company's insurance that proves they carry liability coverage. Both buildings require it before movers can use the elevator or loading areas. Your mover sends it to building management; the building must approve it (often takes 1–2 weeks). It usually must list the building as "additional insured." Ask each building for their COI template and send it to your mover early.
Furniture pickups
When someone buys your furniture (Facebook Marketplace, AptDeco, etc.) and comes to your apartment to take it. You stay home for the agreed window, let them in, and they carry the item out — you don't deliver. Schedule these for weekdays when you're free; buyers often want evenings or weekends, so be clear about your availability. Do pickups at Riverside (where the furniture is now), ideally before Jun 20 so you're not cramming them in after your trip.
